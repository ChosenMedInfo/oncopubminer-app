OncoPubMiner is a web-based platform, user can open the browser anytime and anywhere to search and read articles without any installation operations. It is worth mentioning that the platform is completely free, and the literature data is continuously updated daily. The core goal of the OncoPubMiner is to work with other public platforms to realize the freedom of interpretation of tumor genetic testing data.
Highlights
1. Literatures Online Searching
OncoPubMiner includes the full-text articles in the PMC Open Access subset (~3 million) in addition to the ~30 million abstracts in PubMed.
